About Us

The Dan Baba Couture Story

Dan Baba Couture is a premium fashion brand specializing in high-quality, elegant clothing for both men and women. Since our establishment, we have been committed to excellence in design, quality, and cultural appreciation.

Our workshop uses the finest materials and the expertise of our skilled artisans to create stunning pieces that meet your daily fashion needs while celebrating your unique style.

Our mission is to bring you the finest clothing that honors your cultural heritage while keeping you fashionable and contemporary.

Meet Our Founder

Ibrahim Usman

Founder & Creative Director

With over 15 years of experience in the fashion industry, Dan Baba is the visionary behind Dan Baba Couture. His passion for blending traditional Nigerian aesthetics with contemporary fashion trends has made him a respected name in the industry.

Dan began his journey as an apprentice tailor in Kano, mastering the intricate art of traditional Nigerian garment making. His dedication to craftsmanship and eye for detail quickly earned him recognition, leading to the establishment of Dan Baba Couture in 2010.

Under his leadership, the brand has grown from a small local workshop to a renowned fashion house serving clients across Nigeria and beyond.

"Fashion is not just about clothing; it's about storytelling. Every stitch, every fabric, every design tells a story of our heritage, our culture, and our vision for the future. At Dan Baba Couture, we don't just create clothes - we create wearable art that celebrates identity and elegance."

Dan continues to personally oversee the creative direction of the brand, ensuring that every piece maintains the high standards of quality and artistry that have become synonymous with the Dan Baba Couture name.
